Why Choose StyleStack?

🎨 Professional Typography

EMU-based layout system with baseline grids and professional kerning that rivals InDesign quality within Office applications.

♿ Accessibility Built-in

WCAG AAA compliance automatically enforced with proper contrast ratios, readable fonts, and screen reader compatibility.

Centralized brand management with automatic updates. Change your brand colors once, update everywhere instantly.
